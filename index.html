About.

I am enrolled in the _nology technology consultancy programme to further develop my programming skills that I can take into industry. As part of this programme, I will be advancing my knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, React and Spring Boot. Not only will I learn the technical skills but also apply it in the real world by partnering up with a _nology client to help deliver a sophisticated software solution to their business.


I've completed a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, covering a wide range of modules from Big Data to Compilers. I wanted to gain exposure to the practical way programming is done in industry instead of the theory taught at university and also specialise in a certain area of the technology industry, which is full-stack development.
